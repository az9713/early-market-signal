## Behavior Observed

```text
[User type] are already [new behavior] despite [friction/roughness] because [desired outcome] matters enough.
```


## Counterevidence

Actively look for reasons this is not a startup.

- Is the pain temporary?
- Is it a bug rather than a market?
- Can incumbents patch it quickly?
- Is willingness to pay weak?
- Is the workflow too niche?
- Are users loud but not economically valuable?
- Is the workaround already good enough?
